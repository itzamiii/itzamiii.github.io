Itzamiii! Privacy Policy
Last updated: August 18, 2025

Introduction

Itzamiii! respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Click counts on games – We track the total number of clicks on game entries to measure overall popularity. This data is aggregated and not linked to any individual user.
  • Followed game identifiers – If you enable notifications for games you follow, we store the identifiers (IDs) of those games to send relevant alerts about new offers.
  • Push notification token (FCM) – If you enable notifications, the app obtains and stores a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token from your device. This token is used solely to deliver push notifications and is not linked to your personal identity.

Information We Do Not Collect

This section is provided to explicitly clarify the types of data we do not collect from our users.

  • No personal identification data – We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No payment information – We do not collect credit card details, banking data, or any other financial information.
  • No location data – We do not track your GPS location or store your geographic position.
  • No browsing activity outside the app – We do not monitor or collect information about how you use other apps or websites.

How We Use the Information

  • Improve the app experience by understanding overall game popularity.
  • Send notifications about price updates and new offers for games you marked as “Following” (only if enabled).
  • Use aggregated click counts to power a future feature that allows sorting games by popularity.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We also do not share collected data with advertisers or marketers. Any stored data is used exclusively within Itzamiii! and its backend services.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we store from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We collect only the data necessary for the purposes described above.

Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings. Doing so stops the storage of followed game identifiers and removes the FCM token associated with your device.
  • Because click counts are aggregated and anonymous, they cannot be traced back to you.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or our data practices, contact us at: itzamiii.app@gmail.com
