About this app

Itzamiii! helps you check Nintendo eShop prices, follow games, and find deals for Nintendo Switch worldwide. You can compare prices across different countries, view historical price data, and follow specific games to get notified when their prices change. The app also shows fresh deals as they appear, so you can stay informed about current promotions. You can create custom search tabs to organize the way you browse, filter results by multiple criteria, and sort games according to your preference. You can select from 20 supported countries and view prices in your local currency. The app includes Dark Mode support and works without the need for a user account. There are NO ADS.
